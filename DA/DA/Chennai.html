Popular Destinations in Chennai

  • India

    Marina Beach

    Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, offering stunning sunset views.

  • India

    Kapaleeshwarar Temple

    A stunning example of Dravidian architecture, Kapaleeshwarar Temple is a must-see historical landmark.

  • India

    Fort St. George

    Explore the colonial history of Chennai at Fort St. George, with its museum and historic structures.
