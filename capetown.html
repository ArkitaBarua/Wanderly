Cape Town, South Africa

Where Mountains Meet the Ocean

About Cape Town

Cape Town, the Mother City of South Africa, is a stunning coastal metropolis where natural beauty meets rich history and vibrant culture. Nestled between the iconic Table Mountain and the Atlantic Ocean, this city offers breathtaking landscapes, world-class wineries, and diverse experiences that capture the essence of South Africa.

Must-See Attractions

Table Mountain

Take a cable car ride to the summit of this iconic flat-topped mountain for panoramic views of the city and ocean.

Robben Island

Visit the historic prison where Nelson Mandela was held, now a powerful symbol of freedom and democracy.

Cape Point

Explore the southwestern tip of Africa, where dramatic cliffs meet the merging waters of two oceans.

Local Experiences

Wine Tasting

Sample world-class wines in the historic Stellenbosch and Franschhoek valleys.

Bo-Kaap Culture

Discover the colorful houses and rich Cape Malay culture in this historic neighborhood.

Penguin Watching

Meet the famous African penguins at Boulders Beach in their natural habitat.
